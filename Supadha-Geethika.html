Supadha Geethika.

Nature touches us in all its splendor. It is this nature that makes us feel alive to our sense of being. GEETHIKA is a tribute to that beautiful rhythm of living. Natural, wholesome, joyful.

Open to a life blessed with nature. Your luxury villa at Geethika is set in a pollution-free expanse of Kollur, just minutes away from the hub of shopping and entertainment centers in the city. Come live refreshed.

The 5-bedroom villas are planned across three levels of luxury with care and sensitivity. There are private nooks and family spaces, a home theater, and an entertainment lounge with huge windows and massive sit-outs. It's a celebration of space and luxury!

GEETHIKA is designed to live life inside out. Beyond your villa, the outdoor space invites you to embrace a proactive life. The moment you step out of the villa, you enter a world of surprises. There are children's parks, sprawling landscaped zones, and shaded seats for elders. Colorful blooms peep from the corners.

The Club-Devine at GEETHIKA is an unmatched venue for seeking friends, fitness, and fun. So whether you love swimming or working out, whether you plan to host a party or play an indoor game, whether you are in the mood for solitude or togetherness, we have the space for you.

Club Devine has it all. The pampering things to make you feel special and come alive. The Preview Theater is a whole new experience for the buffs and a private movie screening experience. This is where celebrations are always part of the day's play.

Inspired by nature, it is natural at GEETHIKA to be conscious and sensitive to the finer aspects of holistic living. The specially designed Health Zone caters to your fitness requirements and offers facilities that help you live a healthier and better life.

Step outdoors, and feel nature following you at every step. The designer landscapes with areas for herb gardens and aromatic plants add a certain delight and peace to living. Thoughtful gestures include a pet park, children's play areas, and multiple specially designed activity zones for all age groups.

Active life is a charged life. What better than hitting the day with your favorite sport? Be it a fitness regimen, a friendly game, or a fiercely competitive attitude to train at your game, GEETHIKA integrates this feeling like a melody.

There is quality in thinking, design, planning and execution. Supadha ensures that what you get surpasses your expectations. Every square foot of GEETHIKA bears that insignia that define top quality lifestyle and luxury integrated together with an enthralling expanse of nature all over.

GEETHIKA is brought to you by Supadha Builders, a company steered by a powerful duo of real estate veterans. Together, they have a collectively executed some of the finest gated villa communities and residential apartments across some of the most prestigious locations in Hyderabad. Their expertise in construction, focus on quality, and understanding of customer needs make them one of the highly respected and a trusted name to build a relationships with.
