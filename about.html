Harsha Vardhan Reddy
Chairman/Founder

Harsha Vardhan Reddy, one of the pioneers in the real-estate industry, has over 2 decades of expertise in Properties Marketing Services, Banking, and Finance. He brings on-board a unique vision and management practice for his vast clientele to diversify and grow together. A foundation stands strong with a compatible team and an understanding leader, Harsha Vardhan being an exceptional leader formed a strong base team through the years.

His expertise in the industry led to the inception of Vardhan Realty, Exclusive Real Estate Marketing & Sales Allied Services. It implements a customer-centric approach towards the real-estate market with a stronghold in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Mysore, and Tirupati markets.

Through the years, Vardhan Realty helped customers understand dream homes. He also established Mast Loans - An entity that provides optimum financial solutions for diverse requirements of the customers as well as the builders such as construction loans, developer finance, home loans, mortgage loans, transfer loans, etc.

From providing a new loan to assisting in the transfer of existing loans to lower interest rates, Mast loans helped shape the term financial consultation for all.

Now with his proficiency and enriching industry knowledge, he is instilling an organization named landowners Share Properties with a unique concept of HRCS for the mutual benefit of all the sectors involved in the real-estate market.
