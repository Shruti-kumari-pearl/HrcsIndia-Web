RERA Cosnulting

HRCS provides customers with essential information about properties and property promoters/builders, ensuring compliance with RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) requirements.

We guide customers through RERA guidelines to make their home purchase well-informed and compliant, emphasizing their rights and the benefits of our consulting services.

We assist property promoters in registering their projects under RERA, benefiting both buyers and builders/promoters.

HRCS RERA consulting aims to enhance transparency in real estate transactions, fostering better communication and understanding between buyers and property builders/promoters.
