MRKR's Mera Homes, Krishnarajapura, Bengaluru

Discover serenity and modern comfort at MRKR's Mera Homes, nestled in the heart of Krishnarajapura, Bengaluru. Spanning across an expansive 505 acres, this residential haven offers 476 finely crafted apartments.

Apartment Configuration: Choose from thoughtfully designed 2 & 3 BHK residences, ideal for families of all sizes.

Building Structure: Each tower features a basement, ground floor, and rises gracefully to 14 floors, ensuring panoramic views and ample natural light.

Residential Blocks: Spread across 8 elegantly designed blocks, enhancing privacy and community living.

Location Advantage: Strategically located just off NH 207, MRKR's Mera Homes provides easy access to major routes, making commuting effortless. The tranquil surroundings offer a peaceful retreat amidst the bustling cityscape of Whitefield, Bangalore's celebrated IT hub.

Immerse yourself in a lifestyle that combines convenience with luxury at MRKR's Mera Homes, where every day promises to be filled with blissful moments and cherished memories.
